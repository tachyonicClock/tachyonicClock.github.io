I aspire to create artificial neural networks to reduce tedium and enhance human ability. My specialisation is continual learning, which addresses the technical challenges of continually updating artificial neural networks in response to changes in needs and the world.

- Tutorial at [PAKDD 2025](https://pakdd2025.org/) in Sydney Australia on [Practical Online Continual Learning](https://github.com/adaptive-machine-learning/PAKDD2025).
- Tutorial at [PRICAI 2025](https://www.pricai.org/2025/) in Wellington NZ.
- Research in top tier conferences (A and A\*).
- On the Bridge Committee of The Streaming Continual Learning AAAI Bridge 2026 in Singapore

## Work Experience

_2023 - Now_. **Research Assistant** on the [TAIAO Project](https://taiao.ai/) at Victoria University of Wellington.

- The TAIO project is a data science research program funded by MBIE and conducted in collaboration with several universities and companies; it aims to improve access and provide tools for environmental data science.
- I worked as a part-time research assistant during my PhD.
- Founding contributor and core maintainer of the [CapyMOA](https://capymoa.org/index.html) python library for data stream learning. Over 100 stars!
- Managed community pull requests, releases, and CI/CD for the project.
- Added algorithms and features, notably PyTorch and online continual learning support.
- Presentation at [KiwiPyCon](https://kiwipycon.nz/) 2024 in Wellington NZ on CapyMOA.

_Jul 2021 - 2023._ **Research Assistant** in the [AI Institute](https://ai.waikato.ac.nz/) at the University of Waikato.

* Part-time while as an undergrad.
* Authored a peer-reviewed deep learning research paper to advance the ability of neural networks to learn throughout their lifetime.
* Critiqued and reproduced the latest research to ensure competitive results.
* Engineered experiments with PyTorch to prove the merits of my research.
* Engaged in the AI Institute guest speaker seminars to develop my knowledge and communicated my work internally and externally.

_Nov 2020 - Feb 2021_. **Summer Research Scholar** in [ORCA Labs](https://www.waikato.ac.nz/about/calendar/research-institutes-centres-groups-and-units/orca/) at University of Waikato.

- Implemented a prototype of a distributed, resilient, adaptive system to process depth and colour data for use on a agricultural robot.
- Deployed a Kubernetes cluster on edge hardware empowering ORCA Lab's R\&D.

_Nov 2017 - Jun 2019._ **Programmer** at Nyriad.

- Nyriad was a high-performance computing startup that leverage GPUs to perform parity calculations for data storage. I worked part-time during high school on a team that performed data stream processing in a reproducible and auditable manner.
- Designed, developed, and deployed an internal tool to package internal and external C++ statically compiled libraries using well defined containerised environments, helping Nyriad build products quickly that target multiple environments with minimal compatibility issues.
- Contributed code to many services and components in a variety of languages and frameworks.
